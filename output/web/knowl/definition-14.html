Definition 3.7.1. The Gravitational Field.

The gravitational field created by a spherical object 1  with mass \(m_B\) a distance \(r\) from its center is: \(\vec{g}_{B} = \frac{Gm_B}{r^2}(-\hat{r})\text{,}\) where the universal gravitational constant \(G = 6.67 \times 10^{-11}\frac{\text{m}^3}{\text{kgs}^2}\text{.}\)
