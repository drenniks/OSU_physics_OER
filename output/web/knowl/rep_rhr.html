Representation 1.8.5. The Right Hand Rule.

ADD FIGURE The direction of the vector \(\vec{C}\) that results from the cross product \(\vec{C} = \vec{A} \times \vec{B}\) can be determined using the right hand rule. To use the right hand rule, align your thumb in the direction of \(\vec{A}\text{,}\) your index finger in the direction of vector \(\vec{B}\text{,}\) and your remaining fingers will point in the direction of vector \(\vec{C}\text{.}\)
