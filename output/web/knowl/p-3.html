After successful completion of this chapter, you will be able to:
  1. Explain the difference between a vector and a scalar
  2. List common vectors and scalars
  3. Represent vectors using multiple representations (diagrams, graphs, words, equations)
  4. Resolve a vector into components
  5. Define and use unit vector notation
  6. Add and subtract vectors graphically and mathematically
  7. Multiply a vector by a scalar quantity
  8. Take the dot product between two vectors
  9. Move between the component representation and the magnitude and direction angle representation of vectors.
