Energy comes in many types known as forms. The primary forms you will encounter in physics are kinetic energy (energy associated with motion, including translational, rotational, and thermal kinetic energy) and potential energy (including gravitational, electrical, chemical, and nuclear potential energy). Within a system, energy can transform from one form to another form.
