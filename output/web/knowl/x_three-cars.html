Exercise 4. Explanation - Three Cars.

Consider the following three situations:
  1. You are in a car when the speed limit changes from 35 mph to 45 mph.
  2. You are in a car on the freeway driving at a constant speed of 70 mph.
  3. You are in a car waiting for a red light to turn green.
Rank the three situations by the magnitude of the net force on the car from largest to smallest. Explain your reasoning.
