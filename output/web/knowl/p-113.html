Your friend has developed a new engine that creates carbon dioxide gas. Your friend shows you an equation they created relating the volume of gas created (\(V\)) to the width of the engine (\(w\)), the amount of time the engine has been running (\(\Delta t\)), and the mass of a single molecule of carbon dioxide (\(M\)): \(V = k\frac{w^2\Delta t}{M}\text{.}\) Use what you know about the other symbols in the problem to determine the units of \(k\text{.}\)
