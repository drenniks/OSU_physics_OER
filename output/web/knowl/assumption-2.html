Assumption 2.15.1. Motion with Constant Acceleration.

When you assume an object is moving with constant acceleration in one dimension 1 , you can derive the following equations of motion from the definitions of acceleration and velocity:
\(r_x(t) = r_{xi} + v_{xi}t + \frac{1}{2}a_x t^2\)
\(v_x(t) = v_{xi} + a_x t\)
